# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
Interactive Power BI dashboard built on Maven Analytics' Coffee Shop Sales dataset, analyzing $99K+ in sales across 3 New York City store locations. Covers KPI tracking, product-level performance, hourly demand patterns, and weekday vs weekend sales trends.

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling

## Dashboard Features
- KPI cards: Total Sales, Total Quantity Sold, Total Orders with month-over-month comparisons
- Daily sales trend chart across the selected month
- Sales breakdown by product category and product type
- Store-wise sales performance comparison
- Weekday vs weekend sales split
- Hourly sales heatmap to identify peak business hours
- Interactive calendar filter for period selection

## Key Insights
- Coffee category led all revenue at $38.3K, contributing 30%+ of total sales
- Weekday sales ($73K) outperformed weekend sales ($25K) by nearly 3x
- Peak demand hours were 8AM–10AM consistently across all store locations
- Hell's Kitchen store generated the highest revenue at $33.1K
- Branded products showed the highest month-over-month growth at +40.5%
